Sharad Kelkar is an Indian television actor who shot to fame mainly with his role as Nahar Pratap Singh in Saat Phere - Saloni ka Safar. He also played the role of Rudra Raizada in Sindoor Tere Naam Ka on Zee TV at the same time.

Early life and education

Sharad hails from the city of Gwalior in Madhya Pradesh.[1] He has an MBA in Marketing from Prestige Institute of Management & Research, Indore and is married to fellow actor and co-star Keerti Gaekwad Kelkar.

Career

Sharad Kelkar was a Grasim Mr. India finalist.

Sharad was the host of the show Pati Patni Aur Woh (TV series) on Imagine TV in 2009.[2]

His role as Thakur Digvijay Singh Bhadoria in the show Bairi Piya earned him critical acclaim for his performance.[3]

Sharad also hosted Rock-N-Roll Family along with former Hero Honda Sa Re Ga Ma Pa Challenge 2007 contestant Mauli Dave.[4]

Sharad was also part of Marathi film Chinu where he played the hero to the female protagonist who plays the title role of Chinu.[5]

In 2010, he participated in reality comedy TV series, Comedy Circus Mahasangram (2010) where partnered with Bharti Singh and Paresh Ganatra.[6]

His wife Keerti Gaekwad Kelkar is also a television actress who appeared in Saat Phere as Chandni / Devika in a negative role. Together, the couple also appeared on the dance reality show Nach Baliye 2. His most recent role has been that of Dr. Ashutosh in the serial Kuch Toh Log Kahenge aired on Sony Entertainment Television India.
