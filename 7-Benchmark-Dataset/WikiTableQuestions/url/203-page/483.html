Vitaliy Gennadievich Denisov (Russian: Вита́лий Генна́дьевич Дени́сов; Belarusian: Biтaль Генадзьёвiч Дзянісаў Vital Henadzyovich Dzyanisaw, born 23 February 1987) is a Uzbekistani footballer with Belarusian origins, currently playing for Russian Premier League club Lokomotiv Moscow as a left-back.[1] He is a member of the Uzbekistan national football team.

He is the son of Gennadi Denisov.

Career

Dnipro Dnipropetrovsk

In 2007-2013 he played for Dnipro Dnipropetrovsk in Ukrainian Premier League.

Lokomotiv Moscow

He moved on free transfer to Lokomotiv Moscow and on 14 January 2013 signed 3-years contract with the club.[2] On 5 August 2013 he scored his first goal club for Lokomotiv Moscow on match against Krasnodar.[3] On 26 August 2013 Lokomotiv won Rostov in Russian Premier League match with 5:0 and Denisov assisted in three goals of his club.[4]

In August 2013 Denisov won monthly contest among Lokomotiv's fans and was named Player of the Month.[5]

On 23 December 2013 Denisov was named Uzbekistan Player of the Year.[6]

International

Denisov made his debut in the national team on 22 February 2006 in 2007 AFC AsianCup qualification match against Bangladesh ended with 5:0.
