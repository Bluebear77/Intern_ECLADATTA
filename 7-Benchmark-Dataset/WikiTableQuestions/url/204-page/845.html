2003 Barber Dodge Pro Series season
Previous: 2002 Next: none

The 2003 Barber Dodge Pro Series season was the eighteenth and final season of the series. All races were ran in support of the 2003 CART World Series. The season consisted of ten races of which four were run abroad, one in Mexico and three in Canada. Leonardo Maia won the championship and Colin Fleming won the Rookie of the Year title. This was the first, and only, time the series raced in Mexico.
