Svenska Cupen 1997–98 - Final

Svenska Cupen 1997-98 was the forty-third season of the main Swedish football Cup. The final of the competition took place on 14 May 1998 and was held in Göteborg. Örgryte IS and Helsingborgs IF drew 1-1 before an attendance of 2,559 spectators. The replay was held in Helsingborg a week later with the score also 1-1 with Helsingborgs IF winning 3-0 on penalties. Public interest was much greater for this match with an attendance of 13,092.[1]
