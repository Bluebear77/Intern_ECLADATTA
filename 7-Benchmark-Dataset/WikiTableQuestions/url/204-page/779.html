The following is a list of all Comisión Federal de Telecomunicaciones licensed over-the-air television stations broadcasting in the Mexican state of Morelos.[1][2] There are 8 television stations in Morelos which are independent or affiliated to at least one Televisa, TV Azteca, or Once TV network.
