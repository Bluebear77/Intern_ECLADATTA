The 1998 Philippine Basketball Association (PBA) rookie draft was an event at which teams drafted players from the amateur ranks. The annual rookie draft was held on December 22, 1997 at the Glorietta Mall in Makati City.

Notes

  • Formula Shell Zoom Masters 1st overall pick Danilo Ildefonso was traded to San Miguel Beermen 2nd overall pick Tito Castillo on draft day.
  • Ginebra San Miguel traded the draft rights of Steven Smith to San Miguel Beermen in exchange to Paul Alvarez
  • San Miguel Beermen draft pick William Antonio and Alaska Milkmen draft pick Stephen Antonio are brothers.
