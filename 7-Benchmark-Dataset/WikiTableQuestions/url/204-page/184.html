Boyz n da Hood is the debut album by hip hop group Boyz n da Hood released on June 21, 2005.

The first single from the album was "Dem Boyz", featuring solo verses from Young Jeezy, Jody Breeze and Duke. It is also the most popular; peaking at #13 on the US Rap charts, the group's only single to chart on any Billboard chart. The album has sold about 400,000 copies.
