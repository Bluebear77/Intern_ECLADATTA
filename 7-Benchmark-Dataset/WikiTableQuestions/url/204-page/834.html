The men's 200 metre freestyle competition of the swimming events at the 1975 Pan American Games took place on 19 October.[1][2] The last Pan American Games champion was Frank Heckl of US.[3][4][5][6][7][8]

This race consisted of four lengths of the pool, all in freestyle.[9]
