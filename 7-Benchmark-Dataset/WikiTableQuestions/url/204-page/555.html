Julio René Martínez Sicán (born 27 September 1973) is a Guatemalan race walker.

Personal bests

  • 20 km: 1:17:46 hrs – Eisenhüttenstadt, 8 May 1999
  • 50 km: 3:56:19 hrs – Naumburg, 1 May 2004
