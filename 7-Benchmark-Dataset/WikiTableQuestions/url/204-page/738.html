Arbrå BK is a Swedish football club located in Arbrå.[1]

Background

Arbrå BK currently plays in Division 4 Hälsingland which is the sixth tier of Swedish football.[2] They play their home matches at the Rengsjö IP in Arbrå.[3]

The club is affiliated to Hälsinglands Fotbollförbund.[4]
