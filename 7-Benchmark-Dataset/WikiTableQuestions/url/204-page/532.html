Club de Fútbol Villanovense is a Spanish football team based in Villanueva de la Serena, in the autonomous community of Extremadura. Founded in 1992 it plays in Tercera División – Group 14, holding home matches at Estadio Romero Cuerda, with a capacity of 6,000 seats.
