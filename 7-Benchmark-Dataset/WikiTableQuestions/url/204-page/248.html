Kickboxing for the 2013 Asian Indoor and Martial Arts Games was held at the Dowon Gymnasium.[1] It took place from 3 to 6 July 2013.[2] Previously, in 2009, this sport was contested at both Hanoi (Asian Indoor Games) and Bangkok (Asian Martial Arts Games) separately.[3]
