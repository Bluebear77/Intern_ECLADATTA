Au Printemps (English: In the spring) is Jacques Brel's third studio album. Also known as Jacques Brel 3, the original album was released in 1958. The album was reissued on 23 September 2003 under the title Au Printemps as part of the 16-CD box set Boîte à Bonbons by Barclay (980 816-5).[1][2]
