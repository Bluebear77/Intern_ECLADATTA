The 1970 LPGA Championship was played at Pleasant Valley Country Club in Sutton, Massachusetts. This was the 16th edition of the LPGA Championship and was played from June 12-16.

This championship was won by Shirley Englehorn in an 18-hole playoff over Kathy Whitworth, 74 to 78.
