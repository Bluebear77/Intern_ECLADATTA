The 1990 Superbike World Championship season was the 3rd F.I.M. Superbike World Championship season. The season started on 18 March at Jerez, and finished on 18 November at Manfeild Autocourse after 13 rounds.

Frenchman Raymond Roche won the rider's championship and Honda won the manufacturer's championship.
