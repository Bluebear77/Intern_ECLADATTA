This is a list of Portuguese football transfers for the summer of 2009. The summer transfer window opened on 1 July and closed at midnight, 31 August. Players may have been bought before the transfer windows opens, but may only joined their new club on 1 July. Only moves involving Primeira Liga clubs are listed. Additionally, players without a club may join a club at any time.
