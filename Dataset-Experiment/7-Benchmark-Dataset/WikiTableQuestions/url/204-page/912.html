The men's 500 metres in speed skating at the 1976 Winter Olympics took place on 10 February, at the Eisschnellaufbahn.[1]

Records

Prior to this competition, the existing world and Olympic records were as follows:[2][3]

World record  Yevgeny Kulikov (URS) 36.17 Alma-Ata, Kazakh SSR, Soviet Union 29 March 1975
Olympic record  Erhard Keller (FRG) 39.44 Sapporo, Japan 5 February 1972

The following new Olympic record was set.
