Karina Andrea Koch Benvenuto (born 26 April 1985 in Santiago) is a Chilean tennis player.

Koch Benvenuto has won 11 singles and 19 doubles titles on the ITF tour in her career. On 11 June 2012, she reached her best singles ranking of world number 224. On 11 June 2012, she peaked at world number 289 in the doubles rankings.

Playing for Chile at the Fed Cup, Koch Benvenuto has a win–loss record of 33–29.[1]
