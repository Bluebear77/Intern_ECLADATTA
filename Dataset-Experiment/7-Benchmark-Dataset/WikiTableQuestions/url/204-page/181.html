The following is a list of all Comisión Federal de Telecomunicaciones licensed over-the-air television stations broadcasting in the Mexican state of Chiapas.[1][2] There are 34 television stations in Chiapas which are either independent or affiliated to at least one Televisa, TV Azteca, TV 10 Chiapas network.
