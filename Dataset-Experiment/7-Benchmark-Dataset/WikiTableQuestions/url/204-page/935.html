TVB Anniversary Award for Most Improved Male Artiste
Awarded for "Most improved performance by a male artiste"
Country Hong Kong
Presented by Television Broadcasts Limited
First awarded 1998
Currently held by Vincent Wong (2013)
Official website http://event.tvb.com/

The TVB Anniversary Award for Most Improved Male Artiste is one of the TVB Anniversary Awards presented annually by Television Broadcasts Limited (TVB) to recognize a male artiste who has delivered improving performances in Hong Kong television dramas or variety shows throughout the designated year. Since the introduction of the award in 1998, the official name of the award have changed several times. It was first called the Most Improved On-screen Performance Award—Drama (螢幕大躍進獎—戲劇組) and the Most Improved On-screen Performance Award—Non-drama (螢幕大躍進獎—非戲劇組), separating the category into two awards – one to recognize actors/actresses and another for variety hosts. The award was re-established in 2002 and changed its name to My Favourite Most Improved Male Artiste of the Year (本年度我最喜愛的飛躍進步男藝員). In 2005, the name was changed to Most Improved Male Artiste (飛躍進步男藝員) and has since been using this title.
