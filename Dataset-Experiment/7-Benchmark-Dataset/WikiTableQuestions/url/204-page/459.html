The 2013–14 Chicago State Cougars women's basketball team will represent Chicago State University during the 2013–14 college basketball season. The Cougars, led by eleventh year head coach Angela Jackson, play their home games at the Emil and Patricia Jones Convocation Center as new members of the Western Athletic Conference.[3]
