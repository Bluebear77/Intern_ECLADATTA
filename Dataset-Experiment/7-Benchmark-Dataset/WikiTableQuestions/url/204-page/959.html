2010 season

The 2011 NFL season will contain 3 divisions. A 10 team Division One, 8 team Division Two and a 7 team Division Three. The 2010 Division Two Premiers (Whittlesea) have been promoted to Division One while the Wooden Spooners from Division One (Lalor) relegated to Division Two. The 2010 Division Three Premiers (Hurstbridge) have been promoted to Division Two while the Wooden Spooners from Division Two (Watsonia) relegated to Division Three. The fixture for the 2011 NFL season is not a random selection of matches, but rather is planned by the NFL. The season will run for 18 rounds (including a split round) plus 5 weeks of finals.
