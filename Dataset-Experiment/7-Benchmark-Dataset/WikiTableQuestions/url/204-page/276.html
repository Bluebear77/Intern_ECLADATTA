Adnan Redžović (born March 27, 1977) is a Bosnian super heavyweight kickboxer, boxer, and WKA Thaiboxing European and world champion.[1] He is trained by Jasmin Erović and fighting out of Sports Club Gladijator, Sarajevo, Bosnia and Herzegovina. He made his K-1 debut in 2003 by winning the K-1 BIH "King of Colloseum" 2003 Tournament.[2]

Career

In 2010 Redzovic was signed to It's Showtime and made his debut against Anderson Silva, upsetting Silva with a unanimous decision win. On his next fight in March 2010, he faced Attila Karacs. After an evenly fought three rounds the fight went to extra round and a controversial decision win was given to Karacs.

Redzovic stated that he will be ending his career in K1 after his next fight on May 13 in order to pursue a career in boxing, a sport in which he is undefeated with 3 wins so far. He is in contacts with his friend and current WBA Middleweight Champion Felix Sturm to fight alongside him in a June 25 event.[3]
