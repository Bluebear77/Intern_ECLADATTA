The Diocese of Salford shown within the North of England

This list compiles the entirety of the parishes within the Roman Catholic Diocese of Salford, located in the north of England. Currently, the Rt Revd Terence Brain, the Bishop of Salford leads the diocese.

The list of parishes are each divided by their local authority.
