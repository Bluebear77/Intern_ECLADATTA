Marc Comb is an English Rugby Union player that currently plays provincial rugby for North Otago in the Heartland Championship.

North Otago

Marc Comb joined the North Otago side in 2008 when he came off the bench against Auckland in the Ranfurly Shield encounter. Comb played most of north otago's Heartland Championship matches, starting in a few, but had to miss a couple through to injury.

Luton

Marc Comb played five matches for Luton in the National Division Three South competition from 2007–08. During that time he managed to score ten points (two tries against Dings Crusaders) and was sin binned against Havant.
