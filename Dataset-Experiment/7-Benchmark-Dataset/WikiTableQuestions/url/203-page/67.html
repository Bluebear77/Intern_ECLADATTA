The Primera División de Fútbol Profesional Apertura 2003 season (officially "Torneo Apertura 2003") started on August 2, 2003.

The season was composed of the following clubs:

  • C.D. FAS
  • Municipal Limeño
  • San Salvador F.C.
  • C.D. Águila
  • C.D. Luis Ángel Firpo
  
  • A.D. Isidro Metapán
  • C.D. Atlético Balboa
  • Alianza F.C.
  • C.D. Arcense
  • Chalatenango
