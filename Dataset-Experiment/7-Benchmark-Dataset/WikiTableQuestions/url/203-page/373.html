Main article: Diving at the 1984 Summer Olympics

The Women's 3m Springboard event at the 1984 Summer Olympics was held August 5–6, 1984, at Olympic Swim Stadium in Los Angeles, USA. It was one of four diving events on the Games.[1]

The competition was split into two phases:

  1. Preliminary round (5 August)
    Divers performed ten dives. The twelve divers with the highest scores advanced to the final.
  2. Final (6 August)
    Divers performed another set of ten dives and the score here obtained determined the final ranking.
