Club Deportivo Cudillero is a Spanish football team based in Cudillero, in the autonomous community of Asturias. Founded in 1950 and refounded in 2002 it currently plays in Tercera División – Group 2, holding home games at Estadio La Roja, which has a capacity of 2,000 spectators.

History

Cudillero was founded in 1950 and reformed in 2002 after disappearing in 1979.

Club background

  • CD Cudillero (1950–77)
  • Marino de Cudillero (1980–92, refounded in 2014)
  • CD Pixueto (1994–2000)
  • CD Cudillero (2002–present)
