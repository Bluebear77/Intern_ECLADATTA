Athena Chu (born 25 October 1971) is a Hong Kong actress and singer.

Biography

Chu attended The Hong Kong Academy for Performing Arts from 1990 to 1992, during which time she hosted children's shows on the television station TVB.[1] After graduating in 1992, she started working on films.[1] Her roles with Chow kick-started her film career, and she is considered one of the Sing girls. Later, she moved on to work on television dramas produced by TVB.[citation needed]

After leaving TVB, Chu worked as a model and continued acting in films during the 1990s.[citation needed] In 2000, she started working on Mainland Chinese and Taiwanese television series.[citation needed]
