The Bridgeport Barrage played their second season, as a charter member of the MLL, during the 2002 season of Major League Lacrosse. The Barrage ended up in 3rd place in the American Division with a record of 3-11. The Barrage failed to qualify for the 2002 season MLL playoffs.
