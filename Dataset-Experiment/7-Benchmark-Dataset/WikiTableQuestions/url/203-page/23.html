Daniel Tovar is a Mexican actor (born August 27, 1989). Tovar is mostly known for his roles as Fito in the sitcom Skimo for all its 4 seasons, and as Alejandro in the award-winning film La Zona.[1] However, his career began in 1999 with the miniseries "Camino a Casa".
