Ponant (officially, Compagnie du Ponant (CDP); "Ponant Company") is a French [[cruise ship]] operator. It was founded in April 1988 by Philippe Videau, Jean-Emmanuel Sauvé, and other officers of the French [[Merchant Navy]] and launched the first French cruise ship.<ref>{{cite web|title=L'historique de Compagnie du Ponant|url=http://www.sixstarcruises.co.uk/blog/lhistorique-de-compagnie-du-ponant/|publisher=SixStarCruises|access-date=23 December 2013}}</ref> The company operates eleven ships, all of which operate under the French flag.
