Title: UARFCN

Value:
a uarfcn    abbreviation   for utra absolute radio frequency channel number  where utra stands for umts terrestrial radio access  is used to identify a frequency in the   umts frequency bands    typically channel number is derived from the frequency in mhz through the formula channel number  frequency  5  however  this is only able to represent channels that are centered a multiple of 200 khz  which do not align with licensing in north america  3gpp added several special values for the common north american channels
