1978 Trans-Am season
Previous: 1977 Next: 1979

The 1978 Trans-Am Series season was the thirteenth running of the Sports Car Club of America's premier series. It was the first time the series left North America (i.e. the United States and Canada), with a round in Mexico at the end of the season. All races except for the Six Hours of Watkins Glen ran for approximately one hundred miles.
