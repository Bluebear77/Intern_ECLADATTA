Kasun Nadika Jayasuriya (born 25 March 1980) is a Sri Lankan football player who plays for Renown Sports Club and the Sri Lanka national football team.

He became the top-scorer of Sri Lanka's Kit Premier League, having scored 21 goals. He previously played in the I-League for Indian Bank[1] and Dempo SC.
