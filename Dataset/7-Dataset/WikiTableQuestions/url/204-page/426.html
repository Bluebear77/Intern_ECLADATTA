New York Athletic Club is an American soccer team based in Pelham, New York, United States. Founded in 2008, the team fields clubs in both the National Premier Soccer League and the Cosmopolitan Soccer League (part of the USASA). The two leagues represent the fourth and fifth tiers of the American soccer pyramid, respectively.

The team plays its home games at the New York Athletic Club on Travers Island, where they have played since 2008. The team's colors are red, white and blue.

The women's team, a sister club of the organization, plays in the Women's Premier Soccer League.
