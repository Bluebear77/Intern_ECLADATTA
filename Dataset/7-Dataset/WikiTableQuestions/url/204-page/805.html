The 2009 season is the season of competitive football in Thailand.

The season began on 7 March 2009 for the Premier League and 21 March 2009 for the Division 1. The season finished on 17 October 2009 for the Division 1 and 18 October 2009 for the Premier League.

Diary of the season

  • 1 March 2009: Chonburi won the Kor Royal Cup beating PEA 1-0 at Suphachalasai Stadium.
  • 7 March 2009: The first Premier League matches of the season are played.
  • 18 November 2009: Thailand lost to Singapore at home stadium for first time in 34 years, after they lost 0-1 in 2011 AFC Asian Cup qualification.
  • 23 October 2009: The FA Cup is won by Thai Port on penalties against BEC Tero Sasana after a 1-1 draw at Suphachalasai Stadium.
