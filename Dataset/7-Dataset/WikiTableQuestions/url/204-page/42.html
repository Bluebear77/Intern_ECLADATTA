IFK Visby is a Swedish football club located in Visby on the island of Gotland.[1]

Background

IFK Visby currently plays in Division 4 Gotland which is the sixth tier of Swedish football.[2] They play their home matches at the Västerhejde IP in Visby.[3]

The club is affiliated to Gotlands Fotbollförbund.[4] IFK Visby have competed in the Svenska Cupen on 2 occasions and have played 2 matches in the competition.[5]
