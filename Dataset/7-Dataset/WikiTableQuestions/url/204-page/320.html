2006 Asian Artistic Gymnastics Championships
Host city Surat, India
Dates July 30 – August 3
Main venue Surat Indoor Stadium
<  2003 2008  >

The 2006 Asian Artistic Gymnastics Championships were the 3rd edition of the Asian Artistic Gymnastics Championships, and were held in Surat, India from July 30 to August 3, 2006.
