Señorita Panamá 1995
Date September 1995
Venue Teatro Anayansi Centro de Convenciones Atlapa, Panama City, Panama
Broadcaster RPC (Panama)
Entrants 15
Winner Reina del Carmen Royo Rivera
Panama Central

Señorita Panamá 1995, the 13th Señorita Panamá pageant, was held in Teatro Anayansi Centro de Convenciones Atlapa, Panama City, Panama, September 1995, after weeks of events. The winner of the pageant was Reyna del Carmen Royo Rivera.

The pageant was broadcast live on RPC Panamá. About 15 contestants from all over Panama competed for the crown. At the conclusion of the final night of competition, outgoing titleholder Michele Sage crowned Royo as the new Señorita Panamá.[1]

Royo competed in the 45th edition of the Miss Universe 1996 pageant, which was held at the Aladdin Resort & Casino Theatre, Las Vegas, Nevada, U.S. on May 17, 1996.[2]
