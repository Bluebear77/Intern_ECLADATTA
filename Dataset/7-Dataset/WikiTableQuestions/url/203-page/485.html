Gauri Tejwani (née Pradhan; born 16 September 1977) is an Indian television actress best known for playing the role of Nandini Virani in Ekta Kapoor's Indian soap opera, Kyunki Saas Bhi Kabhi Bahu Thi that aired on Star Plus until 2008.

Early life and education

Gauri Pradhan was born in Pune, Maharastra, India to a Maharashtrian family from the Chandraseniya Kayastha Prabhu community. Her father Subhash Vasudeo Pradhan is a retired Indian Army Officer and her mother was a homemaker. After her father's retirement the family settled in Pune. Her older brother, Bharat, is Petrochemical Engineer and her younger sister Geetanjali, is a doctor. She attended SP College as science major before she left her studies to pursue a modeling career.[1]

Personal life

Tejwani with daughter Katya at the launch of 'Disney Princess Academy'.

Pradhan married her former co-star Hiten Tejwani, on April 29, 2004. They became parents to twins, son Nevan and daughter Katya born on November 11, 2009. [2]

Career

Pradhan started her career with modelling, ramp work and commercials. She has done commercials for many well known companies such as Sprite, Bru, Dabur, Ponds, Santoor, keo karpin, Colgate, Philips and Breeze.[3] In 1998, she came to Mumbai to take part in Miss India and reached among the ten finalists. She made her acting debut with Noorjehan, the Mughal Empress. In October 2001 she rose to fame with Ekta Kapoor's soap Kutumb on Sony TV where she played the lead role of Gauri Mittal. But the Show ended in 2002. Her Chemistry with her co-star was well praised and liked by the audiences that the channel & production house brought the second season of the show along with the same leads. Soon After Kutumb (Season 2), She appeared in another family drama Naam Gum Jayega as the female lead; Priyanka Singh.

After a string of cameos and a short series, In 2004 she was approached again by Ekta Kapoor(producer of her previous successful show Kutumb to play the supporting lead opposite his co-star from Kutumb, Hiten Tejwani in her most-watched No. 1 Show Kyunki Saas Bhi Kabhi Bahu Thi on Star Plus as Nandini. The Role brought claim & fame to Gauri that she won almost the awards of her career for her portrayal in Kyunki Saas Bhi Kabhi Bahu Thi.

She has also made special appearances in other Indian series like Rihaee and Jassi Jaisi Koi Nahin on Sony TV. In March 2005, she entered the popular show Special Squad as the special squad head. In May 2008, she entered the youth oriented show Left Right Left as Shona Das, an army consultant.[4]

In 2009, She did a long cameo in Star Plus's afternoon longrunning hit show Kumkum – Ek Pyara Sa Bandhan as Aditti along with her husband Hiten Tejwani.
