The 2007 Phillip Island Superbike World Championship round was the second round of the 2007 Superbike World Championship season. It took place on the weekend of 2–4 March 2007, at the 4.445 km Phillip Island Grand Prix Circuit in Australia.

Notes

  • In race 2 Max Neukirchner and Michel Fabrizio crossed the finish line at the same time to the nearest thousandth of a second. Fabrizio was initially awarded the position because he started the final lap in front of Neukirchner, but following a review of the official timing system, the FIM decided that Neukirchner crossed the line 15 thousandths of a second ahead FIM press release
