The 2001 CFL Draft took place in the Spring of 2001. 48 players were chosen for Canadian Football League teams from among the eligible CIS football players from Canadian universities, as well as Canadian players playing in the NCAA.[1] Of the 48 draft selections, 26 players were drafted from Canadian Interuniversity Sport institutions.[2]
