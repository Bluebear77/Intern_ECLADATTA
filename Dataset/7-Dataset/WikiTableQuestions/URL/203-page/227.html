The 2008–09 Sacramento Kings season is the 64th season of the franchise, 61st in the National Basketball Association (NBA), 24th in Sacramento.

Key dates

  • June 26: The 2008 NBA Draft will take place in New York City, New York.
  • July 1: The free agency period will start.
