Chemcedine El Araichi (born 18 May 1981 in Mons) is a Belgian-Moroccan football player who plays for Boussu Dour in the Belgian Second Division. In January 2010, he was a free player, because his club R.E. Mouscron was eliminated from the Belgian first division.
