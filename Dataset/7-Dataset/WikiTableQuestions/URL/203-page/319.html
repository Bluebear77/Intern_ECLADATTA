This is a list of hospitals in North Carolina. There are 126 hospitals in 83 counties. Seventeen counties currently do not have a hospital. Duke University Hospital is currently the largest hospital, with 1,002 total hospital beds and operating rooms. Bertie Memorial Hospital is currently the smallest with eight total hospital beds and operating rooms. Charlotte has the highest concentration of hospitals, with seven. Four hospitals serve as Academic Medical Center: Duke University Hospital, Vidant Medical Center, UNC Hospitals and Wake Forest Baptist Medical Center.
