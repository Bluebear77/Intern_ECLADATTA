Since 1997, 50 video games based on the Lego construction system have been released. Originally, games were based completely on Lego properties. In 2001, Lego Creator: Harry Potter introduced games based on licensed, non-Lego intellectual properties. Since then, Lego has licensed several other IPs for use in its video games, including Star Wars, Batman and Rock Band.
