Venue Gostiny Dvor Arcade, Moscow
Broadcaster STS
Entrants 60
Placements 20
Winner Tatiana Kotova
Rostov Oblast

Miss Russia 2006 was held on December 15, 2006 at Gostiny Dvor Arcade. 60 women competed at the pageant where Tatiana Kotova won representing the Rostov Oblast. Zuleyka Rivera and Tatana Kucharova participated in the event. The winner represented Russia at the Miss Universe 2007 and Miss World 2007.
