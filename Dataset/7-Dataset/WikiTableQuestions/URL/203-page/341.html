Polska Liga Koszykówki (PLK) AKA Tauron Basket Liga (TBL) or in English, the Polish Basketball League, is the highest level league of basketball in Poland.

It was previously known as the Dominet Bank Ekstraliga (DBE), the Dominet Basket Liga (DBL), the Era Basket Liga (EBL), and the Lech Basket Liga (LBL).
