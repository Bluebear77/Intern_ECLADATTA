Luis Regueiro Pagola (1 July 1908 – 6 December 1995), sometimes nicknamed Corso, was a Spanish footballer. He played for Real Madrid from 1931 to 1936, scoring 53 goals in 92 matches. He also scored 16 goals in 25 matches for the Spain national football team, including a goal in the 1934 FIFA World Cup against Italy.

After the start of the Spanish Civil War in 1936, he played for the Basque Country national football team which participated in the Mexican League 1938-39 under the name of Euzkadi. Between 1939 and 1942 he played for Mexican club Asturias F.C.,[1] before he finished his career playing for Club América in the Mexican first division.[2]
