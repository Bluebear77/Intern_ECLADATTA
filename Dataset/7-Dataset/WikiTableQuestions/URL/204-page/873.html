The Spouse of the President of the People's Republic of China also the First Lady of China is the wife of the President of China. To date there have been 7 women who have been married to Chinese President whilst in office.

Role and Duties

The role of the Presidential Spouse is not an official office, as they are not given a salary or official duties. Most spouses have remained very much in the background. However, the current spouse Peng Liyuan has gained public attention through her own independent careers and achievements.

Other

Paramount leader

Sometimes the spouse of the paramount leaders is also known as First Lady: Han Zhijun (wife of Hua Guofeng) and Zhuo Lin (wife of Deng Xiaoping).

Spouse of the Head of State of the People's Republic of China (1975–1978)

Spouse of the Head of State of the People's Republic of China under the 2nd Constitution: Kang Keqing (wife of Zhu De) and Li Gang (wife of Ye Jianying)
