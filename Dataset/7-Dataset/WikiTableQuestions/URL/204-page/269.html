The Block Season 9

The Block: Fans vs. Favourites in the eighth season of Australian reality television series The Block, which premiered on Monday, 27 January 2014 at 7:00 pm on the Nine Network. Scott Cam (host) and Shelley Craft (Challenge Master) returned from the previous season, as did the three judges: Neale Whitaker, Shaynna Blaze and Darren Palmer. Production for the series in Melbourne, which was the location for the previous season, in the suburb of Albert Park.

Contestants

This season introduces 2 new couples (dubbed Fans) and 2 previous couples (dubbed favourites). The couples selected were as follows:

Favourites

  • Alisa and Lysandra Fraser, identical twin sisters, winners of The Block: Sky High and former police officers, from Adelaide, South Australia.
  • Brad and Dale, best friends who met on fifth season of the block.

Fans

  • Chantelle and Steve a couple, Dating for two years from Melbourne, Victoria.
  • Kyal and Kara, a married couple of 6 years from the Central Coast (New South Wales).
