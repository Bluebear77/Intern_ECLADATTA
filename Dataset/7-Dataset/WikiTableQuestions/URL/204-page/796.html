The following is a list of all Comisión Federal de Telecomunicaciones licensed over-the-air television stations broadcasting in the Mexican state of Veracruz.[1][2] There are 30 television stations in Veracruz which are either independent or affiliated to at least one Televisa, TV Azteca, Once TV, or RTV+ network.
