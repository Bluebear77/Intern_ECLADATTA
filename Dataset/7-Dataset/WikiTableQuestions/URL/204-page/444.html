Craig Phillips won the first series of the programme in 2000.
Jade Goody who was a housemate in Big Brother 3, Big Brother Panto, Celebrity Big Brother 5 and Bigg Boss 2 died in 2009. She was featured on Ultimate Big Brother as the ultimate housemate.
For the Celebrity Big Brother housemates, see List of Celebrity Big Brother housemates.

Since the start of Big Brother in 2000 there have been a total of 219 civilian, ten Panto, eight teen, 12 Celebrity Hijack and 14 ultimate housemates; 112 men and 125 women. There have been 14 winners of Big Brother; nine men and five women. The youngest winner is Brian Belo, who was 20 at the time of winning Big Brother 8, and the oldest is Cameron Stout, who was 32 when he won Big Brother 4. Winners Nadia Almada and Luke Anderson have both had gender correcting surgery.

The average age of the winners of Big Brother is 25 years old. 16 housemates from Big Brother have been housemates twice, in their own series and Big Brother Panto or Ultimate Big Brother. A total of 177 housemates have been evicted, eight have been ejected, one left due to injury and 19 have walked from the Big Brother House. Only one housemate, Nikki Grahame, has been voted back into the house after being evicted. Nick Bateman and Victor Ebuwa are the only people to have been housemates in three series; Big Brother, Big Brother Panto and Ultimate Big Brother. Jade Goody had also been in three different series and is to date the only civilian housemate to have been a housemate in Celebrity Big Brother, as well as appearing in Big Brother and Big Brother Panto. In 2010, Big Brother 2 winner Brian Dowling was crowned Ultimate Champion over the first 11 series of Big Brother on Channel 4.

The best placed housemate of all time is Brian Belo, placing first out of a record 23 housemates in Big Brother 8. The worst placed housemate is Emily Parr, also of Big Brother 8, who came 23rd after being ejected from the house.

There have been 40 housemates born outside of the United Kingdom, originating from countries such as Ireland, Somalia, Morocco, Portugal, South Africa, Turkey, Poland, Zimbabwe, Italy, China, Canada, Northern Cyprus, Nigeria, Greece, India, Uganda, the United States, Thailand, Egypt, Australia, Sierra Leone, Pakistan, Brazil, Iran and Singapore. Nadia Almada from Portugal, Brian Dowling from Ireland, Brian Belo from Nigeria and most recently Luke Anderson from South Africa have all become winners.

Notable housemates

  • Sada Wilkington becomes the first housemate and woman to be evicted in Big Brother 1.
  • Nicholas Bateman becomes the first housemate to be ejected in Big Brother 1.
  • Craig Phillips becomes the first person and man to win in Big Brother in Big Brother 1.
  • Brian Dowling becomes the first openly homosexual man to enter and win in Big Brother in Big Brother 2.
  • Helen Adams and Paul Clarke become the first housemates to start a relationship inside the house in Big Brother 2.
  • Sunita Sharma becomes the first housemate to walk in Big Brother 3.
  • Kate Lawler becomes the first woman to win Big Brother in Big Brother 3.
  • Jon Tickle becomes the first evicted housemate to be voted back into the house as a guest in Big Brother 4.
  • Paul Brennan becomes the first winner of Teen Big Brother.
  • Ahmed Aghil becomes the first Muslim to enter in Big Brother 5.
  • Nadia Almada becomes the first transgender to win in Big Brother 5.
  • Nicholas Bateman, Melanie Hill, Kitten Pinder, Spencer Smith, Jade Goody, Narinder Kaur, Victor Ebuwa, Anouska Golebiewski, Tim Culley and Marco Sabba become the first housemates to enter the house twice in Big Brother Panto.
  • Nikki Grahame becomes the first evicted housemate to be voted back into the house and was eligible to win in Big Brother 7.
  • Jade Goody becomes the first housemate to enter the house three times in Celebrity Big Brother 5.
  • Amanda and Sam Marchant become the first set of twins to enter the house in Big Brother 8.
  • Lesley Brain becomes the oldest housemate ever in Big Brother 8.
  • Charlie Uchea becomes the first housemate to be fake evicted and return to the house in Big Brother 8.
  • Brian Bello becomes the first black man and youngest housemate to win Big Brother in Big Brother 8.
  • Michael Hughes becomes the first housemate with a disability in Big Brother 9.
  • Keeley Johnson becomes the first housemate to leave due to injury in Big Brother 11.
  • Brian Dowling becomes Ultimate Champion in Ultimate Big Brother.
  • Charlie and Jackie Travers become the first mother and daughter to enter the house in Big Brother 14.
