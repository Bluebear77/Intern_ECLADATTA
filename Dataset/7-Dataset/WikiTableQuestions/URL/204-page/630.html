2008 ARCA RE/MAX Series season
Previous: 2007 Next: 2009

Following are the results of the 2008 ARCA Re/Max Series season. The season began on February 9, 2008 at Daytona International Speedway with the Daytona ARCA 200.

The Series is an American stock car series, the premier division of the ARCA. It is considered a minor, but professional, league of stock car racing, unaffiliated with NASCAR.
