Alberta Provincial Highway No. 566[1] is a highway in the province of Alberta, Canada. It runs west-east between Calgary and Airdrie, from Highway 772 (Symons Valley Road) to Highway 9 between the hamlets of Kathyrn and Keoma.

North of Calgary, from between Range Road 15 & 20 (Panorama Road (24 Street NW) & Mountain View Road) to Range Road 10A (20 Street NE), it is given the designation 176 Avenue N, however it does not enter city limits, with the boundary on the south side of the right of way.

This highway is scheduled for twinning for its entire length.[2]
