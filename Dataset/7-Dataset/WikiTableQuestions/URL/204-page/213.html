<1941–42 1943–44>

The 1942–43 New York Rangers season was the 17th season for the team in the National Hockey League (NHL). During the regular season, the Rangers posted an 11–31–8 record and finished with 30 points.[1] The Rangers finished in last place in the NHL.[2]
