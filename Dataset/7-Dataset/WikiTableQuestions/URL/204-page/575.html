The 2013–14 season was Shrewsbury's second consecutive season back in League One after achieving a 16th placed finish the previous season.[7] They also participated in the League Cup, the Football League Trophy, and the FA Cup.

Season review

League Cup

Shrewsbury Town were knocked out of the League Cup in the first round by Bolton Wanderers who won 3–1 at New Meadow on 6 August 2013.[8]

League Trophy

Shrewsbury Town were knocked out of the League Trophy in the first round by Oldham Athletic who won 1–4 at New Meadow on 3 September 2013.[9]

FA Cup

Shrewsbury Town were knocked out of the FA Cup in the first round by Walsall who won 3-0 at the Bescot Stadium on 9 November 2013.[10]
