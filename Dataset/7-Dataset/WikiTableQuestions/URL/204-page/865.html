The 2007 TPG Tour was a season of golf tournaments on the TPG Tour, the official professional golf tour of Argentina. The season ran from the end of February to the beginning of December, and consisted of seventeen tournaments.

Five events were also part of the Tour de las Americas, the highest level tour in Latin America, and of those, the Coast Open and the Argentine Open were co-sanctioned by the Challenge Tour.

The Order of Merit was won by Rafael Gómez, ahead of Marco Ruiz in second, and Ángel Cabrera in third.
