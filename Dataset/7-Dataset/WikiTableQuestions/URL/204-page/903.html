Sophie Colquhoun
Occupation Actress
Years active 2010 – present

Sophie Colquhoun is an English actress best known for playing Cynthia in the British sitcom Plebs and India in Switch, although she has had minor roles in a number of British comedy programmes, and in the film Captain America: The First Avenger.
