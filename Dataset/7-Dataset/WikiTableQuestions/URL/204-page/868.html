Beatriz Haddad Maia (born 30 May 1996 in São Paulo) is a Brazilian tennis player.

Haddad Maia has won four singles and three doubles titles on the ITF tour in her career. On 22 July 2013, she reached her best singles ranking of world number 265. On 8 July 2013, she peaked at world number 321 in the doubles rankings.

Haddad Maia has a 1–1 record for Brazil in Fed Cup competition.
