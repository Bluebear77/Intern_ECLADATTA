Summary

The Shell Turbochargers find it hard to score victories after another for the 2002 season, after winning over Coca Cola, 90-82 on February 17, the turbochargers lost 8 in a row, and bowed out of contention in the Governor's Cup, won their last game against Sta.Lucia, 95-76 on the last day of eliminations on April 21.

Lost their first three assignments during the Commissioner's Cup, but made it through the quarterfinal round as the 8th seeded team, was ousted by Batang Red Bull. With center Benjie Paras sitting out for most of their games during the season, Shell continued its non-semis appearance in the All-Filipino Cup, lost to Sta.Lucia Realtors in a do-or-die game for the 6th spot in the quarterfinal round.
