This is the complete list of (physical and digital) number-one albums sold in Finland in 2012 according to the Official Finnish Charts composed by Musiikkituottajat – IFPI Finland.

The best-performing album in 2012 in the Finnish chart was 21 by English Adele, spending nine weeks on the top spot. The second-best chart performer was Finnish Jukka Poika with his album Yhdestä puusta spending six weeks atop. The third-best album was Voicen Kesähitti 2012, a compilation of songs by various artists, compiled by TV channel The Voice TV Finland (now known as Kutonen).

The top-ten list of the best-selling 2012 albums in Finland was the following:
