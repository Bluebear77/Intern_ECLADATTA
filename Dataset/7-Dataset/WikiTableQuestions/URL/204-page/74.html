Life Goes On is the fourth studio album by rapper Trae. It was released on October 23, 2007.[4] Guest features include Lil' Wayne, Jody Breeze, Jadakiss, Styles P, Slim Thug, Dallas, Yung Joc, and many more. The first single was "Screwed Up" featuring Lil' Wayne.

The S.L.A.B.-ED version of the album was released on December 8, 2007. DJ Pollie Pop made a MySpace just for the album.[5]

The album sold 12,500 copies in its first week.

Samples

  • "Against All Odds" - Contains a portion of "Deadly Combination", specifically 2Pac's verse.
  • "Nuthin' 2 A Boss" - Contains a sample of "Winter Sadness" performed by Kool and the Gang and "I'm A D-Boy" performed by Lil' Wayne
  • "Smile" - Contains a sample of "Your Smile" performed by Angela Winbush
  • "Give My Last Breath" -The Chorus contains a sample of Trae's earlier single "Swang" part of Big Hawk's rap. The song is a tribute to Big Hawk.
  • "Gittin' High" - Contains a sample of "Sensuality I & II" performed by The Isley Bros and "Wanna Be a Baller" by Fat Pat on the hook.
  • "Ghetto Queen" - Contains a sample of "Living Inside Your Love" performed by Earl Klugh & 2Pac's Pain (Interestingly enough, this is the same sample used on the popular Salad Fingers videos).
  • "The Truth" - Contains a sample of "I Want To Thank You" performed by One Way
