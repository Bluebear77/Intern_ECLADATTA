Championship

In only their second year of participation and fifth conference so far, the Batang Red Bull Thunder has already won a PBA title, with Best import Antonio Lang, the Thunder upset the highly-favored San Miguel Beermen, winners of 5 of the last 6 tournaments, in the finals, 4-2, as Red Bull coach Yeng Guiao won his third championship as a head coach and his first after eight years.
