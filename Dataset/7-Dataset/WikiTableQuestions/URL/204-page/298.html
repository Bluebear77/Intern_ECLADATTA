Johanna Katriina Manninen (born 4 April 1980 in Nurmo) is a former Finnish athlete specializing in sprinting events.

Biography

She competed at 3 Olympic Games, 1996 Summer Olympics, 2000 Summer Olympics and 2004 Summer Olympics.
