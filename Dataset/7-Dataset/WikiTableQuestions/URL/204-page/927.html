Andras Vleminckx, professionally known as Eightysix, is a Belgian record producer. He has produced songs for artists like Kat Deluna[1][2][3]("Drop it Low", "Dancing Tonight"), Taio Cruz ("Positive"), and Tara McDonald ("Give Me More").[4] Dancing tonight reached a #1 spot on the USA Billboard dance chart.[5]
