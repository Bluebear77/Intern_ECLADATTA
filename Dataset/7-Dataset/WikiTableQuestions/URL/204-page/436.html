The 2012–13 Austin Peay State Governors basketball team represented Austin Peay State University during the 2012–13 NCAA Division I men's basketball season. The Governors, led by 23rd year head coach Dave Loos, played their home games at the Dunn Center and were members of the West Division of the Ohio Valley Conference. They finished the season 8–23, 4–12 in OVC play to finish in last place in the West Division. They failed to qualify for the Ohio Valley Tournament
