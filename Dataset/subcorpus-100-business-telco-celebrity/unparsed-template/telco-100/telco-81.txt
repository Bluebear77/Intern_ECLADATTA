{{Broadband subscriptions}}

At the end of 2012 there were estimated to be 6.6 billion mobile network subscriptions worldwide (89% penetration), representing roughly 4.4 billion subscribers (many people have more than one subscription). Growth has been around 9% year-on-year.<ref name=Ericsson-Feb2013>[http://www.ericsson.com/res/docs/2013/ericsson-mobility-report-february-2013.pdf ''Ericsson Mobility Report: Interim Update''], Ericsson, February 2013</ref> Mobile phone subscriptions were expected to reach 9.3 billion in 2018.<ref name=Ericsson-Nov2012>[http://www.ericsson.com/res/docs/2012/ericsson-mobility-report-november-2012.pdf ''Ericsson Mobility Report''] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121202013312/http://www.ericsson.com/res/docs/2012/ericsson-mobility-report-november-2012.pdf |date=2012-12-02 }}, Ericsson, November 2012</ref>
